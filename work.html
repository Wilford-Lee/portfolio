ROS-Powered Industrial Robot Arm for Pick and Place

A collaboration project between Singapore Polytechnic (SP) and Johnson & Johnson (J&J). This project uses Robotic Operating System (ROS) to program and industrial robot arm (Staubli) for a pick-and-place operations on a factory line. Due to cost and development time, an Arduino was used in place of a PLC to control the vacuum suction end effector. A simple use case is shown below by the two GIFs.
